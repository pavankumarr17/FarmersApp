MAIZE
DESCRIPTION
Maize is a leafy stalk whose kernels have seeds inside. It is an angiosperm, which means that its seeds are enclosed inside a fruit or shell.Grows upto 3 m high.

CULTIVATION:
Before maize cultivation Ploughing should be done on a land for to get fine tilth. 
You can also apply 13 to 14 tons of Farm Yard Manure per hectare (F.M.Y) and 10 Azospirillum packets in the field. 
For saving irrigated water you will make the furrow and ridges with 45 cm to 50 cm spacing on a land.

Sowing period:May-Aug        	Harvesting Period:September   -  December
Optimum Temperature:38°C max-10°C min
PH-5.0-7.0
